Legend/Multipliers
EN -    Even Number    - Bet x2
ON -    Odd Number     - Bet x2
RD -    Any Red        - Bet x2
BK -    Any Black      - Bet x2
FH -    Spots 1-18     - Bet x2
SH -    Spots 19-36    - Bet x2
FT -    Spots 1-12     - Bet x3
ST -    Spots 13-24    - Bet x3
TT -    Spots 25-36    - Bet x3
 G -    Green (AKA 0)  - Bet x18
Any singular number grants Bet x36.
The wheel contains 2 green spaces, 18 red spaces, and 18 black spaces (1-36).
